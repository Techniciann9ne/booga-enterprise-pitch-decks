## Slide 2 — Why now

**H:** AI is finally good enough to do the work. Most enterprises still can't use it.

**SUB:** The tools are powerful. The barrier is trust, control, and your knowledge being everywhere but usable.

- Generic AI assistants don't know your business — and you can't safely show them.
- Your most valuable knowledge is scattered across thousands of folders no one can search.
- "Build it ourselves" is a multi-year project most teams can't staff.

**NOTE:** Frame the moment, not the technology. They already believe AI matters; name why they haven't acted yet. ~30 seconds.

---

## Slide 3 — The problem

**H:** Your knowledge is your edge. Today it's trapped.

**SUB:** Decades of documents, precedents, and client history — locked in folders, unsearchable, re-created by hand every week.

- **Trapped** — the answer exists in SharePoint, but no one can find it in time.
- **Expensive** — your highest-paid people spend hours searching and drafting instead of advising.
- **Risky** — every workaround (forwarding files, pasting into a public chatbot) is an exposure waiting to happen.

**NOTE:** Make it personal to the room. [legal example] "You're sitting on decades of SPAs, opinions, and client correspondence. The next great draft is 90% already written somewhere in your archive — but finding it costs a senior associate an afternoon."

## Slide 4 — Meet Booga Agents

**H:** Booga Agents puts AI to work on your knowledge — inside your own walls.

**SUB:** Connect your documents. Booga organizes them automatically. Then agents do the real work — in an environment that's isolated, governed, and yours.

_On-slide diagram label:_ Connect → Understand → Act _(all wrapped in)_ Trust

**NOTE:** The "what is it" slide. Say the one sentence, show the three-verb diagram, promise you'll walk each one. ~45 seconds.

## Slide 5 — Connect

**H:** We come to your data. Your data doesn't come to us.

**SUB:** Connect Booga to SharePoint and Microsoft 365 in minutes. One-way. Read-only. Your system of record never changes.

- **One-way by design.** SharePoint stays the source of truth; Booga keeps a read-only mirror. We never write back.
- **Thousands of folders, automatically.** Point Booga at your document estate; it stays up to date on a schedule.
- **No migration project.** Nothing moves, nothing is re-platformed, nothing is exposed to the public internet.

**NOTE:** This disarms the #1 objection — "are you taking our data?" Land all three words: one-way, read-only, untouched. [legal example] "Your SharePoint stays exactly as it is. Booga reads from it; it can't change it."

---

## Slide 6 — Understand

**H:** Thousands of documents become organized, searchable knowledge — on their own.

**SUB:** Booga reads every file, in every format, in your languages — then proposes how to organize them. You just approve.

- **Reads everything.** Born-digital PDFs, scanned documents, legacy Office files, emails — even decades-old or multi-language archives become clean, searchable text.
- **Organizes itself.** Booga discovers natural groupings across your estate and suggests categories; your team reviews and approves.
- **Stays current.** New documents flow in and get processed automatically — no manual filing, ever.

**NOTE:** The "magic without manual work" slide. [legal example] "A scanned 200-page agreement from 2008, in [Croatian and English] — searchable and organized without anyone touching it. And the filing structure comes from your actual documents, not a generic template." Call out multilingual handling if the firm works in non-English markets.

---

## Slide 7 — Act

**H:** Now your knowledge works for you.

**SUB:** Booga agents do real, repeatable work on top of your organized knowledge — described in plain English, running on their own.

- **Draft client replies, instantly.** An agent watches the inbox, finds the relevant precedent and history, and drafts an informed response for a lawyer to review.
- **Draft documents from your own precedents.** Need an SPA? An agent drafts it from the most similar agreements in your archive.
- **Bring in outside data.** Agents connect to third-party legal data sources and fold the results into the work.

**NOTE:** The emotional peak — "organized documents" becomes "hours of expert time saved." Stress human-in-the-loop: agents draft, professionals decide. "You describe the task once, in plain English. It runs forever." Tee up the live demo.

## Slide 8 — A day in the life _[legal example]_

**H:** One week at a law firm, with Booga.

**SUB:** From a client email at 9:02 to a reviewed draft by 9:15 — here's what changes.

- **9:02** — A client email arrives. The agent pulls the matter history and relevant precedents.
- **9:08** — A grounded draft reply appears in the lawyer's queue, with citations to the source documents.
- **9:15** — The lawyer edits and sends. The insight is remembered for next time.
- **Overnight** — Last night's new SharePoint documents were already read, organized, and made searchable.

## Slide 9 — It remembers

**H:** Most software forgets you the moment you log out. Booga doesn't.

**SUB:** Booga's Intelligence Layer captures the insights, decisions, and context behind the work — and brings them back when they're relevant.

- Every analysis and agent run can leave a memory — confirmed in one click.
- Booga connects those memories and surfaces them in context: _"You flagged a concern about this clause last quarter."_
- The longer your firm uses Booga, the more it knows your practice. The value compounds.

**NOTE:** This separates Booga from "a chatbot on your documents" — it's the moat. The outcome: institutional memory that doesn't walk out the door when a person does. ~30 seconds.

## Slide 10 — Built on trust

**H:** Your data. Your stack. Your rules.

**SUB:** Enterprise isolation, governance, and audit aren't an add-on — they're the foundation.

- **Isolated by architecture.** Your own database, your own search index, your own region — not a shared table with your name on a row.
- **One-way and read-only at the source.** Your systems of record are never modified.
- **Audited end to end.** Every meaningful action is recorded and reportable — GDPR, ISO 27001, and more, on demand.
- **Governed access.** Roles, permissions, and oversight built in from day one.

**NOTE:** The security/procurement slide, delivered to a business audience as peace of mind. "When your security and compliance people get involved — and they will — this is the slide that gets you to yes." Mention data residency if the firm has EU/local requirements.
